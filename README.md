# 📊 Super Market Sales Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and regional profitability across a supermarket business. The dashboard provides actionable insights into revenue generation, customer purchasing patterns, shipping preferences, and business growth trends to support data-driven decision-making.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

The Super Market Sales Dashboard helps business stakeholders monitor key sales metrics, identify high-performing products and customer segments, and evaluate regional sales performance. Through interactive visualizations and KPIs, users can quickly uncover trends and opportunities for business growth.

### Target Users

* Business Analysts
* Sales Managers
* Marketing Teams
* Supply Chain Managers
* Data Analysts

---

# 🛠️ Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Main data visualization platform used for report creation.
* 🔄 **Power Query** – Data transformation and data cleaning layer.
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used for calculated measures, KPIs, and business logic.
* 📈 **Data Modeling** – Relationships established among sales, customer, product, and regional tables.
* 🗺️ **Power BI Maps** – Used for geographical sales and profit analysis.
* 📁 **File Format** – `.pbix` for development and `.png` for dashboard previews.

---

# 📂 Data Source

### Dataset: Super Store Sales Dataset

The dataset contains transactional sales records including:

* Order Details
* Customer Segments
* Product Categories & Sub-Categories
* Sales & Profit Metrics
* Payment Modes
* Shipping Modes
* Regional & State-Level Information
* Delivery Performance Metrics

The data was cleaned, transformed, and modeled using Power Query and Power BI before creating analytical measures using DAX.

---

# ⭐ Features & Highlights

## Business Problem

Retail organizations generate thousands of transactions daily, making it difficult to identify meaningful trends and performance drivers.

Key business questions include:

* Which products generate the highest revenue?
* Which customer segments contribute most to sales?
* Which regions are performing best?
* What payment methods do customers prefer?
* How do sales and profits change over time?
* Which shipping methods are most frequently used?

Without a centralized reporting solution, answering these questions becomes time-consuming and inefficient.

---

## Goal of the Dashboard

The objective of this dashboard is to:

* Monitor overall business performance.
* Analyze sales and profit trends.
* Understand customer purchasing behavior.
* Identify top-performing products and categories.
* Evaluate regional performance.
* Support data-driven business decisions.

## 📈 Monthly Sales Trend

### Key Findings

* Sales consistently increased throughout the year.
* Strong growth was observed during Q4 (October–December).
* December recorded the highest sales volume.
* Year-over-year sales performance improved significantly.

### Business Value

Understanding seasonality helps improve forecasting, inventory planning, and promotional strategies.

---

## 💰 Monthly Profit Trend

### Key Findings

* Profit growth follows a similar trend to sales.
* Q4 generated the highest profitability.
* 2020 profits consistently outperformed 2019.
* Business profitability improved throughout the year.

### Business Value

Profit trend analysis supports budgeting, investment decisions, and financial planning.

---

# 💡 Key Business Insights

### 📌 Revenue & Profitability

* Total Sales reached **1.57M**.
* Total Profit reached **175.26K**.
* Estimated Profit Margin is approximately **11.2%**.
* The business demonstrates healthy profitability and growth.

### 📌 Customer Behavior

* Consumer customers contribute nearly **48%** of sales.
* COD accounts for **43%** of transactions.
* Digital payment adoption continues to grow.

### 📌 Product Insights

* Office Supplies is the highest-performing category.
* Phones are the top-selling sub-category.
* Chairs and Binders generate strong sales performance.

### 📌 Regional Insights

* West and East regions contribute **62%** of total sales.
* South region contributes the least and presents opportunities for market expansion.

### 📌 Seasonal Trends

* Quarter 4 is the strongest sales and profit period.
* Demand peaks during October, November, and December.

### 📌 Logistics Insights

* Standard Shipping dominates customer preference.
* Same-Day shipping has minimal adoption.
